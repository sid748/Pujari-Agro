OUR STORY

FORGED IN INTEGRITY. TRUSTED BY GENERATIONS.

The story of Pujari began in 1960 with our founder, Om Prakash Garg, and a simple promise: to make blades farmers could always rely on. Guided by his father’s wisdom, he committed to creating products that never fail in the field.

For over 60 years, that promise has defined us. From a small workshop to Vijay Industries, Pujari has grown into India’s most trusted name in chaff cutter parts — known for durability, reliability, and trust across generations.

PUJARI OVER THE TIME

DECADES OF CRAFTSMANSHIP

1960
A Vision is Born

Om Prakash Garg begins Pujari with nothing but determination and a dream to manufacture dependable chaff cutter parts.

1970
First Steps Forward

The company establishes its first production unit and gains traction across regional markets in North India.

1980
The Brand Takes Root

Pujari becomes a recognized name among farmers, retailers, and distributors. Demand grows steadily across multiple states.

1990
Expansion of Manufacturing

Vijay Industries invests in larger facilities, advanced machinery, and builds a strong supply chain across central and eastern India.

2000
A Pan-India Presence

Pujari solidifies its position as India’s most trusted blade brand, expanding to states including Maharashtra, West Bengal, Rajasthan, and Bihar.

2010
Technology Meets Tradition

Automation, heat treatment, and new precision systems strengthen production capacity, ensuring consistent quality at scale.

2020
Six Decades Strong

With over 60 years of trust, Pujari stands as a category leader — a brand that farmers ask for by name and distributors trust by legacy.

Parent Company

Cutting-edge solutions.
Rooted in trust

For over three decades, Vijay Industries has been a trusted name in the agri-component space—specializing in high-performance chaff cutter blades and precision-forged parts. With a commitment to innovation, quality, and consistency, we serve a robust B2B network spanning distributors, retailers, and OEMs across India.

Built on a foundation of engineering excellence and customer-centricity, Vijay Industries combines modern machinery with deep market understanding to deliver durable, high-utility components that power rural and industrial India.
